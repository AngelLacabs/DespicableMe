Did you know there are 10,400 Minions living in Gru's house? Wait for the NGL production release for their names and ages!

Minions don't age like humans; they are millions of years old, appearing around the time life began on Earth. While the individual Minions don't age, their lifespan is incredibly long and they are known for being very durable. For example, Bob the Minion is said to be 6 years old in the movie Minions, but this is a movie "age" and his actual biological age is millions of years old.
